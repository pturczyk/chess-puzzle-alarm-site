Wake up and actually get up.

Chess Puzzle Alarm rings like any alarm. To switch it off you solve a chess puzzle. No mindless snooze tapping, so your brain is awake before your feet hit the floor.

An alarm you cannot dismiss on autopilot

The snooze button is too easy. A chess puzzle is not.

Solve to dismiss

The only way off is the right move

When the alarm rings, a chess puzzle appears. Find the winning move and the alarm stops. Pick how hard the morning puzzle should be, from a gentle one-mover to a real tactical test, or drill a theme like forks, pins and checkmates.

Puzzle Rush

Race the clock, three strikes and out

Not just for mornings. Puzzle Rush serves puzzles that ramp in difficulty as you go. Clear as many as you can before three strikes end the run, then chase your best score.

Streaks & history

Keep the run alive

Every solve builds your streak. Track your current run, your best ever, and a full history of how you have been doing, so the habit sticks and the mornings add up.

Achievements

Something to unlock every morning

Milestones for streaks, rush scores and puzzles cleared give you a reason to keep coming back and a way to watch yourself improve over time.
