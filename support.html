Common questions

The alarm did not ring. Chess Puzzle Alarm uses Apple's AlarmKit, which needs permission to schedule alarms. Grant the alarm permission when prompted, keep the alarm enabled, and make sure your device is not powered off at the alarm time.

Do I need an internet connection? No. Puzzles are bundled with the app and everything runs offline.

Where is my data? Everything stays on your device. Deleting the app removes your alarms, history, and settings. See the Privacy Policy.

Which devices are supported? Chess Puzzle Alarm requires iOS 26 or later, because it relies on AlarmKit to ring reliably through Silent mode and Focus.

Chess Puzzle Alarm is an on-device iOS app. It collects no data.
